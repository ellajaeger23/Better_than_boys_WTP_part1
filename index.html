BETTER THAN BOYS

WHERE DEADLINES ARE MADE UP AND THE BUGS DON’T MATTER.

Welcome to the beautiful disaster we call game development. We’re not a giant corporate studio with unlimited funding or a secret AI army. Just a small group of caffeine-fueled dreamers trying to turn chaotic ideas into games people will (hopefully) love.

Why work with us?

  • Fixed a bug only to create three more,
  • Argued with a compiler at 3 a.m.,
  • Said “I’ll clean up this code later” (and lied),
  • Or secretly enjoyed the chaos of game jams—

...then you’ll fit right in.

We’re building games not just to release projects, but to make worlds that are quirky, fun, and maybe a little unhinged, just like us.
