Web Developer

Reference: WD001

Build and ship polished web experiences for our marketing sites and game communities. You’ll collaborate with design and content, and keep our pages blazing fast.

Salary: $50K – $80K (pro-rata for part-time)

Reporting line: Reports to the Engineering Lead

Key responsibilities

  1. Develop, test, and deploy website features and components.
  2. Collaborate with designers to implement responsive layouts.
  3. Optimise performance, accessibility, and SEO.
  4. Fix bugs and participate in code reviews.
  5. Document patterns and share knowledge with the team.

Essential requirements

  • 3+ years with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Comfortable with responsive design and browser quirks.
  • Version control (Git) and basic CLI use.
  • Clear written and verbal communication.

Preferable (nice to have)

  • Experience with a modern framework (React/Vue/Svelte).
  • Basic SEO and analytics familiarity.
  • Accessibility fundamentals (WCAG).

Junior Technician

Reference: TC0A1

Help set up, maintain, and troubleshoot our studio hardware and software so the team can focus on creating. Great starter role with lots of learning.

Salary: $30K – $50K

Reporting line: Reports to the Senior Technician

Key responsibilities

  1. Deploy and configure workstations and peripherals.
  2. Provide first-line support to artists and engineers.
  3. Log, triage, and resolve tickets with supervision.
  4. Maintain asset inventory and update documentation.
  5. Escalate complex issues to senior staff as needed.

Essential requirements

  • Basic knowledge of Windows/macOS setup and updates.
  • Troubleshooting mindset and attention to detail.
  • Comfort communicating with non-technical teammates.

Preferable (nice to have)

  • Exposure to ticketing systems (e.g., Jira, Zendesk).
  • Basic networking (IP, Wi-Fi, VPN, printers).
  • Interest in games or creative tooling.
